Frequently Asked Questions

Once your order is shipped, you'll receive a tracking number via email. You can track it through our website's order tracking page or directly on the courier's website using the provided tracking ID.

We accept all major payment methods including:
  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Rupay)
  • UPI Payments (PhonePe, Google Pay, PayTM)
  • Net Banking
  • Cash on Delivery (COD) - Available for most locations

You can cancel your order within 24 hours of placement:

  1. Go to "My Orders" in your account dashboard
  2. Select the order you want to cancel
  3. Click "Cancel Order" and confirm

Note: Orders that have already been shipped cannot be canceled but may be returned.

Currently, we only ship within India. We're working to expand our shipping options to other countries in the near future. Please check back later or sign up for our newsletter to receive updates.

Returns & Refunds

Return Policy

  • Products must be unused with original packaging

  4. Get refund after quality check
